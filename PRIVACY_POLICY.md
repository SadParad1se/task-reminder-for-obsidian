# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2026

Task Reminder for Obsidian is an Android app that reads TaskNotes tasks from Obsidian vaults selected by the user and schedules local reminders based on task dates. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

## Information the App Accesses

The app only accesses information needed to find tasks and show reminders:

- Obsidian vault folders that you explicitly select using Android's system folder picker.
- Markdown files in the TaskNotes tasks folder within selected vaults.
- TaskNotes plugin settings used to understand where tasks are stored and how task metadata is formatted.
- Parsed task metadata from matching task files, such as task title, excerpt, tags, status, priority, scheduled date, due date, created date, and modified date.
- App settings, such as selected vaults, scan frequency, filter preferences, and notification preferences.

The app does not intentionally collect account information, contact information, precise location, photos, audio, video, payment information, or advertising identifiers.

## How Information Is Used

Information accessed by the app is used only to provide the app's features:

- Scan selected Obsidian vaults for TaskNotes tasks.
- Display, filter, and sort parsed tasks in the app.
- Schedule local Android notifications for scheduled and due task dates, when enabled.
- Open the related task in Obsidian when requested.
- Show local scan status or scan failure notifications.

## Data Storage

Parsed task metadata and app settings are stored locally on your device. The app does not upload this information to any server.

The app is read-only with respect to your Obsidian vault. It does not write to, edit, or delete files in your vault.

## Data Sharing

The app does not sell, rent, transfer, or share your data with third parties.

The app does not include advertising, analytics, tracking SDKs, or remote crash reporting. The app does not send your vault contents, task metadata, or settings to the developer or to any third party.

## Network Access

The app does not require internet access to function and does not use network access to transmit user data.

## Permissions

The app may request or use the following Android permissions and access:

- Folder access: used to read the Obsidian vault folders that you select.
- Notifications: used to show task reminders and scan status notifications.
- Exact alarms: used to trigger task reminders at the scheduled time.
- Receive boot completed: used to restore scheduled reminders after the device restarts.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Parsed task metadata and app settings remain on your device until they are no longer needed, or you remove them.

The app removes locally stored task metadata and cancels related notifications when a task file is deleted, moved out of the scanned TaskNotes folder, no longer matches the configured task criteria, is completed, or when the vault is removed from the app.

You can delete all locally stored app data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage in Android system settings.

## Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted with a new effective date.
